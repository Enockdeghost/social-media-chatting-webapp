Help & Support

Get help with Chatter features and troubleshooting

How do I create an account?

Click the "Sign Up" button in the top right corner and follow the registration process.

How do I post content?

Use the post composer on the home page to create posts with text, images, or hashtags.

How do I follow other users?

Visit their profile and click the "Follow" button, or find them through search.

What are Lists?

Lists allow you to organize users into groups. You can create public or private lists and subscribe to other users' lists.

How do I create a List?

Go to the "Lists" page and click "Create List". Give it a name, description, and choose privacy settings.

How do I add users to a List?

On your list page, use the "Manage Members" tab to search for and add users.

What's the difference between public and private lists?

Public lists can be discovered and subscribed to by other users. Private lists are only visible to you and subscribers you approve.

How do I pin a post to my profile?

Click the three dots menu on any of your posts and select "Pin to Profile". You can only pin one post at a time.

How do I unpin a post?

Click the three dots menu on your pinned post and select "Unpin Post".

Can I pin someone else's post?

No, you can only pin your own posts to your profile.

How do I bookmark a post?

Click the bookmark icon on any post to save it to your bookmarks.

Where can I find my bookmarked posts?

Go to your profile dropdown menu and select "Bookmarks", or visit the Bookmarks page directly.

How do I remove a bookmark?

Click the bookmark icon again on the post, or remove it from your Bookmarks page.
